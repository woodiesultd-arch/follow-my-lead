Meet Your Instructor

Eddie Bateman

With 26 years of dedication to dance, Eddie trained at Fred Astaire South Africa and competed in numerous competitions, achieving a national ranking of #7 in South Africa. His journey has taken him from competitive floors to teaching passionate dancers of all levels.

Eddie is deeply passionate about dance because it builds tremendous self-confidence, boosts fitness, and creates meaningful connections with new people. He loves helping beginners overcome the common myth that "they can't dance" — reminding everyone it's simply left foot, right foot, and rhythm. Anyone can dance!

"Dance isn't about perfection — it's about movement, joy, and connection. Let's hit the floor together!"

Qualifications & Achievements

Professional Credentials

  • Fred Astaire Dance Studios Certification – Completed Bronze Final (2001, 95% grade) and Silver Intermediate (2002, 94.5% grade) levels in Ballroom and Latin American dances through the franchised Fred Astaire training program in South Africa.
  • Competitive Achievements – Achieved a national ranking of #7 in South Africa for competitive Ballroom and Latin events.
  • 26+ Years of Experience – Extensive career as competitor, performer, and instructor, helping dancers of all levels in Auckland build confidence, fitness, and meaningful connections.
  • Teaching Approach – Passionate about proving "anyone can dance" with patient, fun, step-by-step guidance — from complete beginners to advanced social dancers.

These certifications and competitive background provide a solid foundation for delivering professional, high-quality dance instruction.
